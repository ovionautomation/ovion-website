About OVION Automation

OVION Automation is an Industrial Automation & Control Systems company providing end-to-end automation solutions for process industries. Founded by an experienced Automation Engineer with hands-on project experience in pharmaceutical, food & beverage, utility and distillery plants, the company focuses on building reliable, scalable and compliant automation systems.
